This table shows how your pooled phone numbers are assigned to visitors at any given point in time, it does not show incoming calls.

When a call is received, that incoming call will be correlated to the specific visitor/keyword/source listed on this Number Allocation Table at that moment.

This table can also be used as a diagnostic tool to ensure that your phone numbers are not being recycled too rapidly. For example, if every visitor was active within the last 5 minutes, that would suggest that the phone number pool is too small.
